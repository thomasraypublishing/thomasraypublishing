Privacy Policy

Effective: March 20, 2026

Introduction

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC ("we", "us", or "our") built Pomagotchi as a premium application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy — Pomagotchi does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All app data is processed and stored on your device or within your personal Apple ecosystem (iCloud, Game Center).

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not automatically collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our own servers. We do not operate any servers or backend services. Specifically:

  • We do not automatically collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect browsing history (anonymous usage analytics are collected by default — see Anonymous Analytics below)
  • We do not use device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do not use any third-party advertising or analytics services

If you choose to send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally provide your name and email address so we can follow up. This information is sent directly to us via email and is only used to respond to your feedback. We do not store it in any database or use it for any other purpose.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This includes:

  • Your pet's name, stats, and progress
  • Game high scores and trophy achievements
  • Purchased items and coin balance
  • Accessory ownership, equipped accessories, and seasonal expiry dates (shared across all pets)
  • Leveling data (total XP, current level, earned badges, XP history)
  • Competition data (show entries, results, qualifications, season progress, prestige count)
  • Show Wardrobe ownership and equipped show items
  • Redeemed promotional code hashes (one-way hashed, not reversible to the original code)
  • App preferences (sound, haptics, notifications)
  • Diagnostic logs (event counts and session info)
  • Photos saved within the app's Photo Album
  • Location data used for environment detection (see Location Services below)

This data is stored only on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If iCloud Sync is enabled in Settings, some data (pet progress, saved poms, coins, trophies, leveling data, competition data, and show wardrobe) may also sync to your personal iCloud account (see iCloud Sync below).

Location Services

With your permission, Pomagotchi uses your device's location to detect your environment (e.g. home, café, gym, beach, downtown, library, train, airplane, and more) so your pet can react to where you are. Location data is:

  • Processed and stored entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Used only to determine general environment context for pet reactions and location-gated content availability

To identify your surroundings, the App uses Apple's MapKit to query nearby points of interest (such as cafés, gyms, libraries, and restaurants). These queries are sent to Apple's servers and are subject to Apple's privacy policy — we do not receive or store the results on any external server. Venue names returned by MapKit (e.g. a café or restaurant name) are used on-device only to personalize your pet's dialogue, status messages, and dream stories — they are never transmitted to our servers or any third party. The App also uses GPS speed and altitude data (processed on-device) to detect transit modes such as driving, flying, and train travel.

Location is also sent to Apple's WeatherKit service to display current weather conditions for your pet's environment. This request is handled by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings.

iCloud Sync

Pomagotchi can sync your pet's progress, saved poms, coin balance, trophy achievements, leveling data (XP, badges), competition data (entries, results), and show wardrobe items across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This feature is controlled by the “iCloud Sync” toggle in the app's Settings and is enabled by default. When enabled, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account — it is not accessible to us. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's Settings. Disabling it stops all future syncing but does not delete data already stored in iCloud.

Camera, AR & Photos

The Teleport (AR) feature uses your device camera to place your pet in augmented reality. Camera data is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded or transmitted.

The Photo Booth feature lets you save screenshots of your pet. Photos are stored within the app's private storage on your device. You may optionally export photos to your device's Photo Library (Camera Roll), which requires your permission. No photos are transmitted externally.

Motion & Activity Data

Pomagotchi uses your device's motion sensors (via Apple's CoreMotion framework) to detect shakes for playful pet interactions and to count your daily steps for walk bonuses. All motion and pedometer data is processed entirely on your device. No motion data is recorded, stored beyond the current session, or transmitted to us or any third party. You can revoke motion permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Motion & Fitness.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Pomagotchi offers optional in-app purchases and an optional auto-renewable subscription (The Fluff Society) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information. All transactions and subscription billing are handled by Apple subject to their privacy policy.

The Fluff Society subscription provides daily in-app currency rewards and exclusive content (Trading Card theme, Photo Booth scenes, Membership Card). Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit framework. No additional personal data is collected or transmitted for subscription management. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Game Center

Pomagotchi integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Your game scores and achievement progress are submitted to Game Center and may be visible to other Game Center users depending on your Game Center privacy settings. Your Game Center profile and data are managed by Apple. We do not access your Game Center identity beyond what Apple provides for leaderboard and achievement functionality.

Apple Watch

If you use the Pomagotchi Apple Watch companion app, pet data is synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication occurs directly between your paired devices and does not pass through any external servers. The Watch app can also read your step count from Apple HealthKit (with your permission) to trigger walk bonuses for your pet. Step count data is processed on-device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

Widgets, Live Activities, Siri & Spotlight

Pomagotchi supports Home Screen widgets (including interactive Feed and Play buttons), Live Activities, Siri Shortcuts, and Spotlight search indexing to give you quick access to your pet's status. These features use Apple's WidgetKit, ActivityKit, AppIntents, and CoreSpotlight frameworks. Pet data shared with these features stays on your device within Apple's secure app sandbox. Spotlight indexing stores your pet's name, tricks, and badges in the on-device search index — this data is not transmitted externally.

On-Device AI (Apple Intelligence)

On supported devices (iOS 26+), Pomagotchi can use Apple's Foundation Models framework to generate personalized dialogue, dream stories, journal entries, and notification messages for your pet. All AI processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's built-in language model — no text is sent to external servers, and no data leaves your device. This feature is optional and can be disabled at any time in Settings.

On-Device Translation

When your device language is not English, Pomagotchi can automatically translate AI-generated dialogue, status messages, dream stories, and care tips into your language using Apple's Translation framework. All translation happens entirely on your device using downloaded language models — no text is sent to external servers. If the required language model is not installed on your device, translations are not performed and English text is shown instead.

Notifications

With your permission, Pomagotchi sends local notifications to remind you about your pet's needs (hunger, happiness, energy, and cleanliness). Each notification includes a quick action button that lets you address the need directly without opening the app. Expanded notifications display a rich preview showing your pet's current mood and stat levels. Notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device — no push notification servers are involved. Notification content is personalized based on your pet's personality, current weather, environment, and behavioral history (such as app usage patterns and care frequency). The app uses an on-device engagement model to learn when you are most likely to respond to notifications, and personality traits influence notification timing. All behavioral data and timing models remain entirely on your device.

Diagnostics & Feedback

The app maintains a local diagnostics log to help with troubleshooting. This log records event counts and session information on your device only. It is never automatically transmitted. When you send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally choose to include this diagnostic data with your message. The diagnostic report includes your app version, iOS version, device model, and event logs but does not include any personal information. Feedback is sent directly to us via email — we do not use any third-party feedback or support services.

Anonymous Analytics

Pomagotchi collects anonymous usage analytics by default to help us improve the app. The app periodically sends an anonymous usage report to our secure cloud database (Apple CloudKit). These reports are sent at most once every 24 hours.

Reports include: app version, iOS version, device model, device category (iPhone/iPad), screen dimensions, language/locale, anonymous session count, days since first launch, days active in the last 7 and 30 days, pet age (in seconds), pet variant, aggregated action counts (e.g. number of feeds, plays, walks, bedtime uses), game play counts and recent scores, error summaries, which features and settings are enabled (iCloud sync, AI messages, haptics, sound reactions), subscription status and tenure, in-app purchase event counts, promo codes redeemed count, daily streak, trophy count, crash-free session rate, memory warning counts, whether an Apple Watch is paired, and whether widget data is active.

Reports do NOT include: your name, Apple ID, email, location, pet name, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information. Each install is assigned a random identifier for deduplication only — it cannot be used to identify you.

This feature is ON by default. You can disable it at any time in Settings under “Share Anonymous Analytics.” Disabling it immediately stops all future report submissions.

Children's Privacy

Pomagotchi does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No personal data is transmitted to us or to third parties. Location data and all other app data are stored only on the user's device and personal iCloud account. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Pomagotchi does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external services used are Apple's own frameworks — including StoreKit (purchases), Game Center (leaderboards and achievements), ARKit (augmented reality), WeatherKit (weather conditions), MapKit (nearby point-of-interest queries for environment detection), WatchConnectivity (Apple Watch sync), HealthKit (step count on Apple Watch), iCloud Key-Value Storage (cross-device sync), CloudKit (anonymous analytics), SwiftData (on-device structured data persistence), WidgetKit (interactive widgets), ActivityKit (Live Activities), AppIntents (Siri Shortcuts), CoreSpotlight (on-device search indexing), CoreHaptics (tactile feedback), Foundation Models (on-device AI text generation), Translation (on-device language translation), and Swift Charts (score history visualization). All of these are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

The Device - Cosmic Pyramid — Privacy Policy

Effective: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC ("we", "us", or "our") built The Device - Cosmic Pyramid as an entertainment application. This section covers how we handle information when you use The Device - Cosmic Pyramid. We are committed to protecting your privacy. All app data is processed and stored on your device. The only optional cloud features are anonymous analytics and in-app feedback, both of which use Apple's CloudKit and are described below.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not automatically collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our own servers. We do not operate any custom servers or backend services. Specifically:

  • We do not automatically collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect browsing history or usage analytics without your explicit opt-in
  • We do not use device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services

If you choose to send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally provide your name and email address so we can follow up. This information is sent to Apple's CloudKit and is only used to respond to your feedback.

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and the app's Documents directory). This includes:

  • Daily prediction count (resets each day)
  • Purchased prediction balance
  • Total lifetime prediction count
  • In-app purchase transaction IDs (for deduplication)
  • Saved prediction images and reading history (stored as files, capped at 50 readings)
  • Recent prediction values (for diversity tracking)
  • Cached daily horoscope message
  • User preference settings (sound, haptics, notifications)
  • Birth date (optional) — stored locally to personalize readings through zodiac and numerology calculations; never transmitted externally
  • Streak and milestone tracking (consecutive days of use)
  • Subscription status and redemption code activation date
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Widget data (shared via App Group for home screen widget display)
  • Diagnostic logs (event counts and session info)

No data is synced to iCloud, backed up to any server, or shared with any third party.

Location & Weather Data

The Device may optionally request your location to retrieve local weather conditions via Apple's WeatherKit service. This permission is entirely optional — you can deny it and the app functions normally without it. When granted:

  • Your location is sent only to Apple's WeatherKit API to fetch current weather conditions.
  • Weather data is used solely to flavor prediction narratives (e.g., storm-themed intros during rainy weather).
  • Your location is never stored, logged, or transmitted to Thomas Ray Publishing or any third party.
  • You can revoke location access at any time in Settings > Privacy > Location Services.

On-Device AI Processing

The Device uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models and NaturalLanguage framework extensively to personalize your experience. When AI Predictions is enabled in Settings, the on-device model generates: creative prediction options for each category, unique poetic narrative intros for each prediction, reading summaries that tie your predictions into a cohesive story, mystical reasons for your lucky numbers, atmospheric peril prompts, personalized daily notification messages, enhanced Siri responses, and reflective commentary when revisiting past readings. Daily horoscope messages and prediction diversity analysis also use on-device AI. All AI processing occurs entirely on your device. No prompts, inputs, or outputs are transmitted to any external server. Apple's on-device models do not send data to Apple or any third party. You can disable AI Predictions at any time in Settings.

Saved Prediction Images

When you save a prediction reading, an image is rendered and stored in the app's local Documents directory on your device. You may choose to share this image using the iOS share sheet, which is handled entirely by the operating system. Thomas Ray Publishing does not have access to shared images or their destinations. You control what you save and share.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

The Device offers optional in-app purchases including a consumable single-prediction pack and auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and annual) for unlimited readings. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Thomas Ray Publishing does not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is stored locally on your device.

Notifications

The Device may request permission to send local push notifications. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and include daily reading reminders and moon phase alerts. No notification data is sent to any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings or in iOS Settings.

Siri & App Intents

The Device provides Siri Shortcuts that let you start a reading, check your cosmic streak, view your last prediction, or check the moon phase using voice commands. When you invoke a shortcut, Siri sends your request to the app, which processes it entirely on your device. No voice data or intent data is transmitted to Thomas Ray Publishing or any third party.

Live Activities

The Device uses Live Activities to display prediction progress on your lock screen and Dynamic Island during readings, and optionally shows a persistent cosmic status display with moon phase, streak count, and reading availability. All Live Activity data is generated and displayed locally on your device. No Live Activity data is transmitted externally.

Home Screen Widget

The Device includes an optional home screen widget that displays moon phase, reading availability, streak count, and your daily horoscope. Widget data is shared between the main app and the widget extension via an App Group container stored locally on your device. No widget data is transmitted externally.

Diagnostics & Feedback

The app maintains a local diagnostics log to help with troubleshooting. This log records event counts and session information on your device only. It is never automatically transmitted. When you send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally choose to include this diagnostic data with your message. The diagnostic report includes your app version, iOS version, device model, and event logs but does not include any personal information. Feedback is submitted to Apple's CloudKit — we do not use any third-party feedback or support services.

Anonymous Analytics

The Device collects anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app. The app periodically sends an anonymous usage report to our secure cloud database (Apple CloudKit). These reports are sent at most once every 24 hours.

Reports include: app version, iOS version, device model, screen dimensions, language/locale, anonymous session count, total readings count, current and longest streaks, subscription and redemption status, purchased prediction count, feature toggle states (sound, haptics, notifications, Enhanced Awareness), AI model availability, engagement counts (quick readings, shares, share bonuses, gallery size), onboarding status, free readings remaining, purchase event count, attunement completion rate, average reading interval, most frequent prediction category, device language, sound cache performance, AI generation speed, days since install, session duration, paywall views, time-of-day usage patterns, Apple Watch companion app usage, aggregated action counts, and error summaries.

Reports do NOT include: your name, Apple ID, email, location, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information. Each install is assigned a random identifier for deduplication only — it cannot be used to identify you.

This feature is ON by default. You can disable it at any time via “Share Anonymous Analytics” in Settings. Disabling it immediately stops all future report submissions.

Children's Privacy

The Device does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No personal data is transmitted to us or to third parties unless you explicitly opt in to anonymous analytics or submit feedback. The app is suitable for all ages as an entertainment product.

Colophon — Privacy Policy

Effective: March 17, 2026

Introduction

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC ("we", "us", or "our") built Colophon as a business management application for independent publishers, authors, and small creative businesses. This section covers how we handle information when you use Colophon. We are committed to protecting your privacy — Colophon does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All business data is processed and stored on your device or within your personal Apple ecosystem (iCloud).

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not automatically collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our own servers. We do not operate any custom servers or backend services. Specifically:

  • We do not automatically collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect browsing history or usage data without your explicit opt-in
  • We do not use device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services
  • We do not connect to banking systems or access account balances
  • We do not send data to external AI services

If you choose to send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally provide your name and email address so we can follow up. This information is stored in Apple's CloudKit and is only used to respond to your feedback.

Data Stored on Your Device

All business data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This includes:

  • Expense records (merchant, amount, date, category, notes, receipt images)
  • Mileage trip logs (origin, destination, distance, purpose)
  • Business documents (files, tags, categories, expiration dates)
  • Business activities and calendar event references
  • Journal entries (text, voice transcriptions, attendees, action items)
  • Book titles and royalty records from publishing platforms
  • Revenue tracking data by book and platform
  • Funding account records (display name, institution name, optional last-four digits, payment method type)
  • Capital contributions and tax basis calculations
  • App preferences and settings
  • Diagnostic logs (event counts and session info)

This data is stored only on your device. If iCloud Sync is enabled, your data also syncs to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit (see iCloud Sync below).

Funding Accounts & Financial Data

Colophon allows you to track funding accounts for business and personal finance separation. Account records store only a display name, institution name, optional last-four digits of the account number, and payment method type. Colophon does not connect to any bank, access account balances, or process any financial transactions. Capital contribution and tax basis calculations are performed entirely on your device. No financial data is transmitted to Thomas Ray Publishing or any third party.

Location Services

With your permission, Colophon uses your device's location for mileage trip detection and address-based distance calculations. Location data is:

  • Processed and stored entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Used only for mileage logging and IRS deduction calculations

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings.

iCloud Sync

Colophon syncs your business data across your Apple devices using Apple's NSPersistentCloudKitContainer. When enabled, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account — it is not accessible to us. Sync includes expenses, trips, documents, activities, journal entries, royalty records, and funding account data. You can manage iCloud sync through your device's iCloud settings.

Calendar Integration

With your permission, Colophon reads events from your Apple Calendar (via EventKit) to link business activities to calendar entries. Calendar data is read on-device and cached locally. Colophon may create new calendar events when you explicitly request it but does not modify or delete existing events without your action. Calendar data is never transmitted to Thomas Ray Publishing or any third party.

Camera & Photos

Colophon uses your device's camera to capture receipt images. The app also uses Apple's Vision framework to perform on-device OCR (optical character recognition) to extract merchant names, amounts, and dates from receipts. You can also import receipt images from your Photos library. All image processing happens entirely on your device — no images or extracted data are transmitted externally.

Voice Recording & Transcription

The Voice-to-Journal feature uses your device's microphone and Apple's Speech framework to transcribe voice recordings into journal entries. All transcription occurs entirely on your device. Audio recordings are not stored permanently unless you save the transcription as a journal entry. Thomas Ray Publishing does not receive or store any audio data. You can revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

On-Device AI Processing

Colophon uses Apple's on-device machine learning frameworks (Vision, NaturalLanguage, and Foundation Models) for receipt OCR, expense categorization, journal entity extraction, and natural language business queries. All AI processing happens entirely on your device — no prompts, inputs, or outputs are transmitted to any external server.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Colophon offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and annual) for advanced features. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Thomas Ray Publishing does not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit 2 framework.

Siri & App Intents

Colophon provides Siri Shortcuts for quick access to expenses, mileage, reports, and other features. When you invoke a shortcut, Siri sends your request to the app, which processes it entirely on your device. No voice data or intent data is transmitted to Thomas Ray Publishing.

Diagnostics & Feedback

The app maintains a local diagnostics log to help with troubleshooting. This log records event counts and session information on your device only. It is never automatically transmitted. When you send feedback through the app's Feedback form, you may optionally choose to include diagnostic data. The diagnostic report includes your app version, iOS version, device model, and session history but does not include any personal or business data. Feedback is submitted to Apple's CloudKit.

Anonymous Analytics

Colophon offers optional anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app. Unlike our other apps, analytics in Colophon are disabled by default and require your explicit opt-in. When enabled, the app periodically sends an anonymous usage report to our secure cloud database (Apple CloudKit), at most once every 24 hours.

Reports include: app version, iOS/macOS version, device model, screen dimensions, language/locale, anonymous session count, feature usage counts (number of expenses, trips, documents, journal entries), entity counts, sync health status, subscription status, and crash-free session rate.

Reports do NOT include: your name, Apple ID, email, location, business data, financial figures, merchant names, document contents, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information. Each install is assigned a random identifier for deduplication only.

You can enable or disable analytics at any time in Settings under “Share Anonymous Analytics.”

Children's Privacy

Colophon is a business management application designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Colophon does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external services used are Apple's own frameworks — including Core Data with CloudKit (data storage and sync), StoreKit 2 (subscriptions), EventKit (calendar integration), Vision (receipt OCR), Speech (voice transcription), NaturalLanguage (entity extraction), Foundation Models (on-device AI), Charts (financial visualization), AppIntents (Siri Shortcuts), and PDFKit (report generation). All of these are governed by Apple's privacy policy.
