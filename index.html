About Us

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC is an independent publisher bringing creative stories, art, and digital experiences to life. From illustrated children's books to art collections and interactive apps, we craft experiences that spark joy and imagination.

Based on a love for Pomeranians, art, and storytelling, our publications span the playful adventures of Captain Henry Gingersnaps to curated visual art collections and our virtual pet companion app, Pomagotchi.
