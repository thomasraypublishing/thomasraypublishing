Pomagotchi

Your Virtual Pomeranian Companion

Adopt and care for your very own virtual Pomeranian. Feed, play, and bond with your fluffy companion in this delightful iOS app from the world of Thomas Ray Publishing.

  • Raise and nurture your own virtual Pom
  • Play mini-games and earn rewards
  • Level up from 1 to 33 — earn XP from mini-games, daily care, and trophies
  • Enter Dog Show Competitions across 4 tiers — Regional, State, National, and International
  • Equip premium Show Wardrobe items to boost your judging score
  • Dress up your Pom with hats, glasses, and collars from the Closet
  • Collect seasonal holiday accessories throughout the year
  • Redeem exclusive promotional codes for bonus coins and rare accessories
  • Watch your pet react to 16 real-world environments — cafés, gyms, beaches, downtown, and more — with venue-specific dialogue and first-visit discovery reactions
  • Place your Pom in augmented reality with Teleport — save AR photos to your in-app gallery
  • Puff Pop! match-3 with 999 levels, Cat Nap Challenge bonus rounds, time limits, and lives
  • Capture memories in the Photo Booth and create Trading Cards — free!
  • Apple Intelligence — on-device AI dialogue, AI-generated Photo Booth scenes & Trading Card backgrounds
  • 10 Siri Shortcuts with entity queries — ask Siri about your pet's stats, tricks, and badges
  • Smart Care Siri shortcut — ask Siri to suggest the best care action
  • Personalized care tips based on your pet's personality and behavior
  • Intelligent notification timing that adapts to your schedule
  • Re-engagement notifications with personality-flavored messages
  • Spotlight search indexing for your pet, tricks, and badges
  • Sync across all your Apple devices

The Device - Cosmic Pyramid

A Mystical Fortune Telling Experience

Gaze into the triangular glass brick pyramid and receive predictions from beyond the stars. A mystical fortune telling app with on-device AI, immersive visuals, and cosmic energy.

  • Custom mystical fonts — Orbitron titles, Cormorant Garamond narratives, Space Mono readouts
  • Enhanced 3-layer parallax starfield with mystical smoke wisps
  • Narrative prediction intros with inclusive, gender-neutral language
  • Save and share prediction results as images
  • Past Readings gallery to revisit your saved predictions
  • AI-powered daily horoscope using Apple Foundation Models (on-device)
  • AI-generated prediction options tailored to cosmic mood and context (on-device)
  • AI-generated narrative intros, reading summaries, lucky number reasons, and gallery reflections
  • Dynamic peril prompts, notification messages, and Siri responses powered by on-device AI
  • NaturalLanguage-powered prediction diversity for varied readings
  • Cosmic Context system — moon phase, weather, season, and device signals influence predictions
  • Stereo sound engine with cathedral reverb, cosmic transitions, and ambient drones
  • Zodiac & numerology personalization — enter your birth date for Western zodiac, Chinese zodiac, and Life Path readings
  • 4 Siri Shortcuts — start a reading, check your streak, view your last prediction, or check the moon phase
  • Live Activities on lock screen and Dynamic Island during readings
  • Persistent Cosmic Status Live Activity with moon phase, streak, and availability
  • Home screen widget with moon phase, daily horoscope, streak, and readings count
  • Optional monthly and annual subscriptions for unlimited readings
  • Streak and milestone tracking for daily cosmic engagement
  • WeatherKit integration for location-aware cosmic readings (optional)
  • Requires iOS 26 for on-device AI features

About Us

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC is an independent publisher bringing creative stories, art, and digital experiences to life. From illustrated children's books to art collections and interactive apps, we craft experiences that spark joy and imagination.

Based on a love for Pomeranians, art, and storytelling, our publications span the playful adventures of Captain Henry Gingersnaps, curated visual art collections, our virtual pet companion app Pomagotchi, the mystical fortune telling experience The Device, and a lineup of iMessage sticker packs for everyday delight.
