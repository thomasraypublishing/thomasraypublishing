IVAbout the House

An independent publisher
for small wonders.

We make curious little things — books, art volumes, and apps that feel like they arrived from a nearby, fluffier universe.

Thomas Ray Publishing LLC is an independent publisher bringing creative stories, art, and digital experiences to life. From illustrated children's books to art collections and interactive apps, we craft experiences that spark joy and imagination.

Rooted in a love for Pomeranians, art, and storytelling, our catalog spans the playful adventures of Captain Henry Gingersnaps, curated visual art collections, our virtual pet companion Pomagotchi, the mystical Device, and a lineup of iMessage sticker packs for everyday delight.
