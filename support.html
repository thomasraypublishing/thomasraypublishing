Pomagotchi — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I dress up my Pom with accessories?

Tap the pink Closet button in the scroll menu to open your Closet. From there you can equip hats, glasses, and collars on your Pom. Purchase accessories from the Seasonal tab in the Shop — year-round basics are always available, and seasonal holiday items appear during their corresponding month. You can wear one item per slot (hat + glasses + collar) simultaneously. Accessory ownership is shared across all your Pomeranians.

Do seasonal accessories expire?

Yes. Seasonal holiday accessories expire after purchase — the sooner of 30 days or 15 days after the holiday month ends. Year-round basic accessories are permanent and never expire. You can see how many days remain on each seasonal item in your Closet.

How do I sync my pet across devices?

Pomagotchi uses iCloud to sync your pet's progress, saved poms, coins, and trophies across your Apple devices. iCloud Sync is enabled by default — you can toggle it on or off in the app's Settings. Make sure iCloud is also enabled on your device in Settings under your Apple Account > iCloud.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app and go to Settings. Your purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored automatically. If items don't appear, try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the shop.

How do I manage or cancel my Fluff Society subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How do I enable or disable location features?

Go to your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Pomagotchi. You can choose to allow location access "While Using the App" or "Never." Your pet will still function without location access, but environment-aware scenes (café, gym, beach, downtown, library, etc.) and weather-based reactions will be disabled.

Is Pomagotchi safe for children?

Yes. Pomagotchi does not collect any personal information and does not connect to any third-party services. All data stays on your device or in your personal iCloud account. The app is safe for users of all ages.

How do I share my diagnostics for troubleshooting?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll to the Diagnostics section, and tap "Export Diagnostics." You can then share the report via email. The report contains only app and device information — no personal data.

How do I redeem a promotional code?

Open the Shop (either the Pom Pom Shop or Fluffy Puffy Services tab) and scroll to the bottom. You'll see a "Super Secret Fluff Pom Code" section where you can enter your code and tap Redeem. Each code can only be used once per device. Rewards such as coins and exclusive accessories are granted instantly.

How does the leveling system work?

Your player level ranges from 1 to 33. You earn XP by playing mini-games, maintaining daily care streaks, unlocking trophies, entering competitions, and logging in daily. Each level requires progressively more XP. Leveling up earns rewards like Pom Coins, treat packs, badges, and exclusive Show Wardrobe items. Your level is global — it carries across all your Pomeranians.

What are Dog Show Competitions?

Dog Show Competitions let you enter your Pom in judged events across four tiers: Regional, State, National, and International. Each tier requires a minimum player level and an entry fee in Pom Coins. Your Pom is scored based on recent mini-game performance, trophy collection, pet care, and equipped Show Wardrobe items. You compete against AI-generated opponents, and top-3 finishers earn prizes and qualify for the next tier.

How do I qualify for higher competition tiers?

Regional competitions have no qualification requirement — just reach level 10 and pay the entry fee. To enter State competitions, you need a top-3 finish at Regional level. National requires a top-3 at State, and International requires a top-3 at National. You also need to meet the level requirement for each tier (State: 16, National: 23, International: 30).

What is the Show Wardrobe?

The Show Wardrobe is a separate outfit system for competitions. It includes four slots: Collar, Headpiece, Body Accessory, and Special Effect. Each item has a quality tier (Standard, Premium, Elite, or Champion) that provides a multiplier bonus during competition judging. Champion-tier items are exclusive rewards from competition victories. You can browse and equip Show Wardrobe items from the Pom Show hub.

Are competition entry fees refundable?

No. Entry fees (paid in Pom Coins) are deducted when you enter a competition and are not refunded regardless of your placement. Plan your entries strategically based on your current stats and Show Wardrobe items.

Where can I learn more about the Pom Show?

Open Settings > Pom Show Guide for a detailed overview of the leveling system, competition tiers, judging criteria, Show Wardrobe, and tips for success. The guide explains everything from how XP is earned to how attire multipliers affect your competition score.

The Device - Cosmic Pyramid — Frequently Asked Questions

What are the narrative intros on predictions?

Each prediction in The Device is introduced by a short poetic narrative rather than a plain category label. These intros set a mystical tone and use inclusive, gender-neutral language — no assumptions are made about your gender, orientation, or family structure. The narratives replace the original category labels (like "Love" or "Career") with evocative phrasing that draws you into the reading.

How does the daily horoscope work?

The Device generates a personalized daily horoscope message using Apple's on-device Foundation Models. The horoscope considers the time of day, your reading history, moon phase, season, and local weather (if location is enabled). It is generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. The horoscope is cached for the day so you see the same message each time. If the on-device AI model is not available on your device, The Device falls back to a curated set of horoscope messages.

What is the Cosmic Context system?

The Device gathers ambient cosmic signals — moon phase, season, time of day, solstice proximity, and optionally your local weather — to influence prediction narratives and option weighting. For example, during a full moon you may see moon-themed narrative intros, and stormy weather may bring more mystical predictions. Device signals like screen brightness also contribute to the cosmic alignment score. All of this happens locally on your device.

Why does The Device ask for my location?

Location is used only to fetch local weather conditions via Apple's WeatherKit. Weather data adds flavor to your predictions (e.g., storm-themed intros on rainy days). This is entirely optional — you can deny the permission and The Device works perfectly without it. Your location is never stored or sent to Thomas Ray Publishing.

How do I save and share my readings?

After a prediction reveal, you will see Save and Share buttons on the reveal screen. Tap Save to store the reading image to your Past Readings gallery, which you can access from About > Past Readings. Tap Share to share the prediction image using the standard iOS share sheet (messages, social media, email, etc.). Saved images are stored locally on your device.

Why does The Device require iOS 26?

The Device uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models to power its AI-generated daily horoscope and Apple's NaturalLanguage framework for prediction diversity. These on-device AI capabilities require iOS 26. All AI processing happens locally on your device for maximum privacy — no data is sent to any server.
